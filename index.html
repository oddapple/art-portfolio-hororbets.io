Art & Design

I’ve discovered Adobe Photoshop in 2009 and was using it primarily for in-game screenshot retouching. Since then I expanded my expertise, creating collages, posters, banners, book-illustrations, social-media templates for posts and all sorts of things. Currently I use it primarily to make my own digital art.

I’ve never worked in this field full-time, but I’ve done some freelance work taking commissions. Most of my projects are personal and I would love to challenge myself with something commercial.

My biggest accomplishments are minicomix “Forest” and series of stickers, both are sampled in portfolio.

Past Work Experience & Education

I have a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, but last two job positions that I’ve got were not accounting-related. In 2017 I worked at an international wedding agency and since 2018 till 2019 I was an English teacher in a private school.

Currently I am expending my skill set by picking up some programming.
